Understanding the Metrics
Accuracy

Overall correctness: (TP+TN)/(Total)

Precision

Of flagged fraud, how many were correct: TP/(TP+FP)

Recall

Of actual fraud, how many were caught: TP/(TP+FN)

F1-Score

Harmonic mean of precision and recall

ROC-AUC

Ability to distinguish between classes

TN (True Negative)

Legit correctly identified

FP (False Positive)

Legit incorrectly flagged as fraud

FN (False Negative)

Fraud missed by the model
